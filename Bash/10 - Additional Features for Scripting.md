## 10.7 Redefining Commands with alias
* `alias `*`new_name`*`=`*`command`*
* `unalias `*`alias_name`* : remove an alias
  * `unalias -a` removes all aliases
* `\`*`command`* : disables aliases this time
* arguments are not allowed in aliases
